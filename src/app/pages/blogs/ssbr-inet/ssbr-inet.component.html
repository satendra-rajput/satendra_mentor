Code_Lekhak is a freemium educational platform website designed to help you learn coding online. Initially released in December 2019, it derives its name from the World Wide Web of the Internet, though it is not affiliated with the Code_Lekhak consortium. Code_Lekhak works tirelessly to provide comprehensive courses covering all aspects of web technologies development, making it a one-stop solution for aspiring developers or those looking to enhance their skills


Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, Code_Lekhak also publishes free HTML templates and web templates that you can easily integrate into your projects. Plus, with our built-in online text editor called "Try it Yourself," you can edit examples or run the code for testing right on the platform. Code_Lekhak is committed to continuous innovation, regularly developing new courses based on extensive research to stay ahead of the curve.
